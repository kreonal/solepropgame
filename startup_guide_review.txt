STARTUP GUIDE REVIEW
====================
Current text vs. proposed rewrites. Changes noted with reason.


STEP 1 — Welcome
-----------------
Icon: 👟
Title: Welcome to Sole Proprietor [KEEP]
Subtitle: The core loop [KEEP]

CURRENT body:
"You run a sneaker resale shop. Each day, customers walk through the door to buy, sell, and trade."

PROPOSED body:
"You run a sneaker resell shop. Customers come through every day to buy pairs from you, sell you their shoes, and trade."

Reason: "resale" -> "resell" is more natural sneaker community language. Reordering gives BUY first since that's your primary revenue.

CURRENT points:
- BUY: "They want a pair from your inventory."
- SELL: "They have shoes to offload."
- TRADE: "They'll swap pairs with you, with optional cash on top."

PROPOSED points:
- BUY: "A customer wants to buy a pair from your inventory."
- SELL: "A customer wants to sell you their shoes."
- TRADE: "A customer wants to swap pairs with you. Cash can be added either way to make it fair."

Reason: More explicit about who is doing what. Removes ambiguity on TRADE cash direction.

CURRENT footer:
"End the day when you're done with customers. Between days, adjust your prices and prepare for tomorrow."

PROPOSED footer:
"End the day when you're done. Between days, set your prices, post your social media photo, and get ready for the next one."

Reason: References the new photo post feature. More specific about what "prepare" means.


STEP 2 — Time
--------------
Icon: ⏱
Title: 10 Hours Per Day [KEEP]
Subtitle: "Time is your most limited resource" [KEEP]

CURRENT body:
"Every customer interaction costs time. When the clock runs out, the shop closes — regardless of how many customers are still waiting."

PROPOSED body:
"Every customer costs time. When the clock hits zero the shop closes, no matter how many people are still in line."

Reason: Shorter, punchier. No em dash.

CURRENT points:
- BUY: "0.25h — quick transaction"
- SELL: "0.5h + optional 0.5h to closely inspect for fakes"
- TRADE: "0.5h + optional 0.5h to closely inspect for fakes"

PROPOSED points:
- BUY: "0.25h per transaction"
- SELL: "0.5h base, plus 0.5h if you closely inspect for fakes"
- TRADE: "0.5h base, plus 0.5h if you closely inspect for fakes"

Reason: No em dashes. Clearer phrasing.

CURRENT footer:
"Use 'Not Interested' on SELL or TRADE customers to skip them for free. Prioritize the customers that move the needle."

PROPOSED footer:
"Hit 'Not Interested' on any SELL or TRADE customer to skip them with zero time cost. Be selective. Not every customer is worth your time."

Reason: "move the needle" feels corporate. More direct and has personality.

STEP 4 — Storage + Inventory
------------------------------
Icon: 📦
Title: Limited Storage [KEEP]
Subtitle: "Inventory management matters" [KEEP]

CURRENT body:
"Your shop can only hold 50 pairs to start. A full shop means you can't buy anything until you sell."

PROPOSED body:
"Your shop holds 50 pairs to start. A full shop means you can't take in anything new until you move some product."

Reason: "move some product" feels more natural for the context.

CURRENT points:
- "List prices: Set between days, but is locked during the day"
- "Upgrade storage space: Purchase more space as you build your sneaker empire"

PROPOSED points:
- "List prices: Set them between days. Once the shop opens they're locked in."
- "Storage upgrades: Buy more space in the Store tab as your business grows."

Reason: Grammar fix on first point. References "Store tab" by its actual name now (renamed from Growth). Removes "sneaker empire" which feels corny.

CURRENT footer:
"Use the marketing tools between days to shape who walks through the door."

PROPOSED footer:
"Post your daily social media photo between days to guarantee walk-ins. The Store tab has upgrades to help you scale."

Reason: References the actual photo post feature by name. Removes vague "marketing tools" language.


STEP 5 — Releases + Raffles
-----------------------------
Icon: 🎟
Title: Releases & Raffles [KEEP]
Subtitle: "New drops start in Week 2" [KEEP]

CURRENT body:
"Every week after Day 7, new shoes drop. You can enter raffles or secure a full size run with a preorder."

PROPOSED body:
"Starting Week 2, new shoes drop every week. Enter a raffle for a shot at retail, or lock in a full size run with a preorder."

Reason: Clearer timeline. "lock in" is more natural sneaker language.

CURRENT points:
- Raffle: "Win a pair at retail if you're selected"
- Preorder: "Guaranteed full size run at 120% retail"
- Demand: "New releases bring extra buyers the next day"
- Schedule: "Nike · Jordan · Adidas weekly — New Balance (even weeks) · Asics (odd weeks)"

PROPOSED points:
- Raffle: "10% chance to cop a pair at retail price"
- Preorder: "Guaranteed full size run at 120% of retail"
- Demand: "Fresh releases bring extra buyers through the door the next day"
- Schedule: "Nike, Jordan, and Adidas drop every week. New Balance on even weeks, Asics on odd weeks."

Reason: Raffle now correctly reflects the flat 10% win chance (old "hype tier" language is gone). Schedule removes the em dash. Demand copy is more vivid.

CURRENT footer:
"Hype tier matters — Grail releases are rare but highly profitable. Low-buzz drops are easier to win but margins are tighter."

PROPOSED footer:
"Grails are rare and secondary-market only. Regular releases are your bread and butter. Win them, flip them, build the bag."

Reason: Hype tiers were removed from the game entirely. Grails are now secondary market only per the unified catalog system. Updated to reflect actual game logic. No em dash.


====================
SUMMARY OF KEY CHANGES
====================
1. Removed all hype tier references (no longer in the game)
2. Updated raffle win rate to reflect flat 10% (no more hype-based probability)
3. Added reference to the social media photo post feature
4. Renamed "Growth tab" to "Store tab" everywhere
5. Updated grail description to secondary market only
6. Removed em dashes throughout
7. Tightened language to sound less corporate/AI, more like a real indie game
